St Patrick's Day

When is the holiday?

On 17 March

What is the holiday for?

St. Patrick's Day observes of the death of St. Patrick, the patron saint of Ireland. The holiday has evolved into a celebration of Irish culture with parades, special foods, music, dancing, drinking and a whole lot of green.

What special things do people do?

Eating corned beef and cabbage and bacon! Rocking shamrocks,Drowning the shamrock,Enjoying some,Looking for leprechauns Guinness,Wearing green,Pinching anyone who isn't wearing green,Wearing blue.

What special food do people eat?

Colcannon,Irish Soda Bread,Fried Cabbage,Corned Beef and Cabbage,Shepherd's Pie,Irish Stew,Irish Apple Tart,Barmbrack.
